Why Migrate to the Cloud?

No matter the size of your company, if your business is using on-premises servers, you’ll be in the process of or keen to make the transition to virtual servers in the cloud so you can enjoy on-demand, shared resources to compute, store, and use network services at scale. The cloud is ideal for companies and start-ups racing against the clock to design, build and market new products and services fast and efficiently.

Strategies for Successful Cloud Migration

Essential Cloud Migration Steps with Profisea

1

Assessment and planning

At this stage, we evaluate existing IT infrastructure and identify which workloads and apps should be optimized for the cloud. This includes determining each workload's performance, security, and associated regulatory or compliance requirements. Only after this assessment do we develop a detailed migration plan that sets out your timeline, resources, and budget.

2

Preparation

Before starting the transition, it is crucial to verify that your existing IT infrastructure is correctly prepared for the migration. This includes optimizing workloads, configuring network and security settings, and ensuring proper data backup.

3

Migration

Moving workloads and apps to the cloud is the actual migration. Depending on the complexity of the workloads, we use a set of automated cloud data migration services and DevOps tools.

4

Testing and validation

We perform tests on the migrated workloads and applications to verify that they are secure, operational, and reliable.

5

Post-migration optimization

At this stage, we optimize the cloud environment to ensure its peak performance by fine-tuning the network and security settings and ensuring that all required updates and patches are deployed.

6

Continuous monitoring

Finally, we constantly monitor and manage the cloud environment. Best DevOps practices involve the utilization of best cloud migration professional services, including tracking and alerting tools, security checks, and the timely application of updates and patches.
